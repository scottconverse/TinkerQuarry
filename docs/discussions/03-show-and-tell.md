# Show & Tell: post your first TinkerQuarry print

Made something? Show it off. This is the place for prints, prompts, and happy accidents.

**A good Show & Tell post:**

- 📸 **A photo** of the printed part (on the bed or in the wild).
- 💬 **The prompt** you used — exact words. Prompts that worked well help everyone.
- 🖨️ **Your printer + material** (e.g. Bambu P2S, PLA).
- ✅ **The readiness score** TinkerQuarry gave it, and whether it printed first try.
- 🔧 Any **parameter tweaks** you made in the panel.

**Copy-paste template:**

```
**What it is:**
**Prompt:** "…"
**Printer / material:**
**Readiness:** XX/100
**First-try?:** yes / no — what I changed:
```

To kick it off — a classic functional first part:

> **What it is:** a desk cable clip
> **Prompt:** "a desk cable clip for an 8 mm cable"
> **Printer / material:** Bambu Lab P2S, PLA
> **Readiness:** 92/100
> **First-try?:** yes — printed as designed (16 × 27 × 10 mm)

Bonus points for the *weird* ones — the brackets, jigs, organizers, and one-off fixes that are
exactly why functional 3D printing is great. Show us what you quarried. ⛏️
